Kenneth Maluleke, known professionally as Master Kenny is a South African musician  and record producer.
Born in Xihoko Village and raise in Bolobedu Ga Modjadji, Lenokwe Village. He is also known as the pioneer
of "Bolobedu" dance. Musical career Genres Electro - Afropop - House Occupation(s) Music producer - DJ -
Vocalist Years active 2011 - present
